# Neon Bluff — Support

Thanks for playing Neon Bluff.

## Quick help

**The colour name and the colour of the card don't match — what do I do?**
That's the whole game. Tap when the *word* matches the *colour* of the card. If they disagree, don't tap.

**There's a coral border with "DON'T TAP" — does that mean I shouldn't tap?**
Right — that's INVERT mode. The rule flips. Tap only when the word and colour *don't* match.

**The screen flashes a lot. Can I dial it down?**
A reduced-intensity option is on the roadmap for the next update. iOS *Settings → Accessibility → Motion → Reduce Motion* is honoured today and softens most animations.

**My high score reset.**
High scores are stored on your device. Deleting and reinstalling the app clears them. They are not synced to iCloud or to a server.

**Can I turn off ads?**
A paid no-ads option is being considered for a future update. Today the free version is the only version.

**Game Center isn't showing my score.**
Make sure you're signed into Game Center at *iPhone Settings → Game Center*. Game Center sign-in is optional — leaderboards only update if you're signed in.
